Serves 4

Ingredients

  1. 3 slices (-360g) raw salmon
  2. dash salt
  3. dash pepper
  4. 1/4 head (300g) cabbage
  5. 1/2 (100g) onion
  6. 1/2 (100g) carrot
  7. Vegetable Oil (A little:)
  8. 1 tbsp sake
  9. 1 and 1/2 tbsp sugar
  10. 3 tbsp miso
  11. 20g Butter
  12. 1/2 tbsp Soy Sauce

How To Cook

  1. Lightly salt and pepper the salmon. Roughly cut the cabbage. Cut the onion into 1 cm thick slices and the carrot into thin rectangles.
  2. In a heated frying pan, ad vegetable oil. Cook salmon startin with the skin side, until colored, 2-3 minutes.
  3. Flip fish. Add cabbage, onion, and carrot. Add sake, pouring around the pan. Add miso and sugar here and there. Add small pieces of butter on top of this miso.
  4. Cover with aluminium foil and steam 6-7 minutes.
  5. Take off the foil. Mix. Add soy sauce.
